What is the purpose of this feature?

This feature allows you to evaluate a progress of your choice (distance, time, ...) from the beginning of a year to a specific day of that year. And compare it to previous years. Let's take an example: Today is {{todayMoment.format("MMM Do")}}. So the feature will reply to the following question:

"What I've accomplished by {{todayMoment.format("MMM Do")}} of this year compared to previous years to the same date?"

How to display progression in imperial system unit?

You will find a "App system units" option in common settings. Set it to imperial and go back to this page
